H2: Curriculum
H3: Start thinking like a Data Scientist
Paragraph: This course will help experienced and novice managers alike to upgrade their skill set to be effective in the modern data-centric organisation. You will learn to gather, interpret, visualise and summarise data using a variety of software. We introduce basic data science tools including Excel for cleaning and wrangling, Power BI for reporting and live dashboards, and Jupyter and Python for predictive modelling, further data visualisation and time-series forecasting.
Paragraph: By the end of this module, you will be able to answer the question "What is Data Science". We sill cover the principles of Data Science, and the most recent trends. Thereafter, we will move on to cleaning and manipulating data in Excel
Terminology, technologies, team sizes, history of data science, potential savings and efficiencies, examples.
Types of tabular data, numerical vs. categorical data, outliers and missing values, using Excel effectively, pivot tables.
Paragraph: In this module, we'll go over some basics, and move onto visualisation and modelling.
Measures of central tendency, measures of spread, descriptive vs. inferential statistics, summary statistics, types of plots and graphs, creating visuals in Power BI, creating live dashboards.
Understand and fit a regression line to data, classification vs. regression problems, extrapolating time-series data, building a simple predictive model.

H2: FAQ
H3: You have questions; we have answers
FAQ_0: 


What is the learning portal?




Our critically-acclaimed online learning platform, ATHENA, ensures the timeous delivery of all your course material; keeps track of your progress on coursework and tests; and enables contact between you and the EXPLORE facilitators for when you find yourself in a pinch.


FAQ_1: 


Will I have enough time?




Our courses are designed to fit in with the busy lives of family-runners and businesspeople. They require less than 90 mins per day during weekdays, so the weekends can be used to do any additional reading or catching up, as required.


FAQ_2: 


Are there any learning pre-requisites?




We don't have any material requirements for existing qualifications. However, an analytical background as well as 3+ years experience in leading teams of multiple people would certainly help you to benefit as much as possible from the course.


FAQ_3: 


Is there any specific hardware or software required?




All you need to complete the course is access to a computer and an internet connection. We provide instructions for both Windows and Mac operating systems, where necessary, and there are no hidden charges for additional software or applications.


FAQ_4: 


Is the course accredited?




EXPLORE is SETA accredited and our data science course is at NQF Level 5. Upon completion of the course you will receive a certificate.
